<p><ul><li>Single Luciferase Assay Reagent</li><li>Double-luciferase Assay Reagent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The luciferase assay reagent market is divided into two primary types: single luciferase assay reagents and double luciferase assay reagents. Single luciferase assay reagents measure the activity of one luciferase enzyme, providing straightforward insights into gene expression or cellular activity. In contrast, double luciferase assay reagents utilize two luciferase enzymes, enabling comparative analysis of promoter activity or normalization of results, which enhances accuracy and reliability in experimental outcomes. These distinctions cater to diverse research needs in molecular biology.</p></p>
