<p><p>The flexible packaging films market comprises several key types: </p><p>- **PE (Polyethylene)**: Known for its moisture resistance and versatility, widely used in food and beverage packaging.</p><p>- **LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene)**: Offers flexibility and low-density characteristics, ideal for bags and wraps.</p><p>- **PP (Polypropylene)**: Known for its durability and heat resistance, commonly used for food containers.</p><p>- **PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate)**: Provides strength and visibility, frequently used in beverage bottles.</p><p>- **PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride)**: Ensures excellent barrier properties, often utilized in pharmaceuticals and food packaging.</p></p>
