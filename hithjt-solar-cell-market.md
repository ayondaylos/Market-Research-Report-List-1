<p><p>The HIT (Heterojunction with Intrinsic Thin layer) solar cell market is primarily categorized into two types: monofacial and bifacial cells. Monofacial HIT cells generate electricity from one side and are widely used in residential and commercial applications due to their efficiency and compact design. In contrast, bifacial HIT cells capture sunlight from both sides, enhancing energy production, especially in reflective environments. This dual functionality makes bifacial cells increasingly popular for utility-scale projects, as they can maximize energy yield.</p></p>
