<p><strong>The Ripening Culture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mesophilic Cultures</li><li>Thermophilic Cultures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ripening culture markets are categorized into mesophilic and thermophilic cultures based on temperature preferences for the fermentation process. Mesophilic cultures thrive at moderate temperatures (20-30°C), commonly used in cheeses like cheddar and gouda, enhancing texture and flavor. In contrast, thermophilic cultures operate at higher temperatures (40-65°C) and are essential in producing cheeses such as mozzarella and parmesan, promoting a more robust fermentation. Both culture types play a crucial role in cheese ripening, influencing taste, aroma, and quality.</p></p>
