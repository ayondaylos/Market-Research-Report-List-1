<p><p>The retinal disease therapeutics market encompasses treatments for various eye disorders, including macular degeneration, diabetic macular edema, diabetic retinopathy, and retinal vein occlusion. Macular degeneration affects central vision, primarily in older adults. Diabetic macular edema involves fluid accumulation due to diabetes, impairing vision. Diabetic retinopathy is a diabetes-related condition causing retinal damage. Retinal vein occlusion is a blockage of blood vessels in the retina, leading to vision loss. These conditions drive demand for innovative therapies, prompting market growth.</p></p>
