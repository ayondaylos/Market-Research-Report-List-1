<p><p>The chemical respirators market is classified into several types: </p><p>1. **Disposable Respirators** are single-use masks that protect against airborne particles.  </p><p>2. **Air Purifying Respirators (APRs)** filter contaminants from the air using replaceable cartridges.  </p><p>3. **Powered Air Purifying Respirators (PAPRs)** use a battery-powered fan to draw air through filters, enhancing comfort.  </p><p>4. **Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBAs)** provide breathable air from a tank, used in hazardous environments.  </p><p>5. **Other market** includes specialty respirators designed for specific industries or applications.</p></p>
