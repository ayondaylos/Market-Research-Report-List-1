<p><ul><li>Dry Etching Equipment</li><li>Wet Etching Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor etching equipment market is primarily divided into two types: dry etching equipment and wet etching equipment. Dry etching involves the use of gases in a vacuum environment to precisely remove materials from semiconductor wafers, allowing for intricate patterning essential in advanced chip manufacturing. In contrast, wet etching utilizes liquid chemicals to etch away layers on the wafer surface, offering a simpler and cost-effective approach for less complex applications. Both methods are critical for shaping semiconductor devices during fabrication.</p></p>
