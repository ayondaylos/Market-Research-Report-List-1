<p><p>Non-negative pressure water supply equipment includes systems designed to maintain consistent water delivery pressure. The two primary market types are steady flow and adjustable tank systems. Steady flow systems provide a constant water output, ensuring reliable supply without fluctuations, ideal for applications requiring stable pressure. Adjustable tank systems, on the other hand, enable variable flow rates by managing water levels in storage tanks, allowing for flexibility to meet changing demand. Both types enhance efficiency and sustainability in water distribution networks.</p></p>
