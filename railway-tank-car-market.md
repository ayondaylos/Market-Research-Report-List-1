<p><p>The railway tank car market comprises two main types: pressurized and non-pressurized tank cars. Pressurized tank cars are designed to safely transport gases or volatile liquids under high pressure, preventing leaks and maintaining integrity. Non-pressurized tank cars, on the other hand, are used for transporting liquids that are less volatile and do not require pressure containment. Each type is crucial for the efficient and safe transport of various commodities, contributing significantly to the overall logistics and supply chain of the rail industry.</p></p>
