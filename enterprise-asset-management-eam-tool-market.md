<p><p>Enterprise Asset Management (EAM) tools are categorized primarily into two market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based EAM tools offer flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT maintenance costs, allowing organizations to access asset data remotely. They typically operate on a subscription model. In contrast, on-premises EAM solutions involve installing software locally on company servers, providing greater control over data security and customization, though they require higher upfront costs and ongoing IT support. Both options cater to different organizational needs.</p></p>
