<p><p>The hydraulic fracturing fluids market consists of three main types: water-based fluids, foam-based fluids, and gelled oil-based fluids. Water-based fluids, predominantly used for their cost-effectiveness and availability, typically consist of water mixed with additives to enhance performance. Foam-based fluids utilize a combination of gas and liquid, providing improved fluid characteristics and reduced reservoir damage. Gelled oil-based fluids, featuring gelled hydrocarbons, offer excellent viscosity and control in high-temperature conditions, making them suitable for specific geological formations.</p></p>
