<p><strong>The NMP Recycling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Freeze Recovery</li><li>Wheel Recovery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NMP (N-Methyl-2-pyrrolidone) Recycling System market comprises two primary types: Freeze Recovery and Wheel Recovery. Freeze Recovery involves cooling NMP to solidify impurities, allowing for easy separation and purification of the solvent. In contrast, Wheel Recovery employs a thermal treatment method where NMP is circulated through a rotating wheel setup, enhancing solvent recovery efficiency. Both methods aim to minimize waste and promote sustainability in industries using NMP, such as electronics and pharmaceuticals, by reclaiming valuable resources.</p></p>
