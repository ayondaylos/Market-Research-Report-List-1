<p><p>Small business ERP software can be categorized into two main market types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises ERP requires businesses to install and maintain systems on their own servers, often providing greater control and customization but demanding higher upfront costs and IT resources. In contrast, cloud-based ERP software is hosted on the vendor's servers, offering accessibility from anywhere, lower initial investment, and automatic updates. Cloud solutions are increasingly popular for their scalability and flexibility, aligning well with the needs of small businesses.</p></p>
