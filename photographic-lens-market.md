<p><ul><li>Prime Lens</li><li>Zoom Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photographic lens market is primarily categorized into prime and zoom lenses. Prime lenses have a fixed focal length, offering superior image quality and low-light performance, making them ideal for portrait and street photography. In contrast, zoom lenses provide variable focal lengths, allowing for versatility in framing without changing lenses, which is beneficial for dynamic shooting environments like events or travel. Both types serve distinct needs, catering to different preferences and shooting styles among photographers.</p></p>
