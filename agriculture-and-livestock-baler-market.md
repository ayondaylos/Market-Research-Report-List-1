Round balers produce cylindrical bales, which are typically easier to handle and ideal for larger farms, as they can be efficiently stored and transported. Square balers, on the other hand, create rectangular bales that are easier to stack and manage, making them suitable for small to medium-sized operations. Both types play a crucial role in forage harvesting and livestock feed management.</p></p>
