<p><p>The Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) hardware market comprises various components essential for effective imaging and analysis. Magnets generate the strong magnetic field required for NMR. Field locks stabilize this field, while shim coils enhance uniformity. Sample probes hold the samples safely during analysis. RF coils transmit and receive radiofrequency signals to excite nuclei and detect resonances. Gradient coils are responsible for spatial encoding, allowing for detailed imaging. Lastly, quadrature detectors enhance signal detection efficiency, improving overall system performance.</p></p>
