<p><ul><li>Thermal Type</li><li>Quantum Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IR sensors market encompasses two primary types: thermal and quantum. Thermal IR sensors detect heat emitted by objects, providing a measure of thermal radiation, making them ideal for applications like imaging and temperature measurement. Quantum IR sensors, on the other hand, leverage quantum effects to detect specific wavelengths of infrared light, offering higher sensitivity and faster response times. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, healthcare, and security, addressing varying needs for environmental monitoring and detection.</p></p>
