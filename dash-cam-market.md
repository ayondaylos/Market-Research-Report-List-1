<p><strong>The Dash Cam Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel Dash Cameras</li><li>Multi-Channel Dash Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dash cam market is primarily divided into two types: single-channel and multi-channel dash cameras. Single-channel dash cameras capture video from a single perspective, typically facing the road ahead, making them ideal for basic monitoring and recording incidents. Multi-channel dash cameras, on the other hand, feature multiple lenses to record from various angles, such as the front, rear, and sides of a vehicle, providing comprehensive coverage and enhancing safety by capturing a wider range of events and potential collisions.</p></p>
