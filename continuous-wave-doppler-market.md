<p><p>Continuous Wave Doppler (CW Doppler) is a medical imaging technique that utilizes ultrasound waves to measure blood flow and assess cardiovascular function. It operates by emitting continuous sound waves, which reflect off moving blood cells, allowing for the evaluation of blood velocity and flow dynamics in real time. This technology is crucial for diagnosing various cardiovascular conditions and plays a significant role in obstetrics, particularly in fetal monitoring.</p><p>The Continuous Wave Doppler Market is witnessing substantial growth, driven by the rising prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, increasing geriatric populations, and advancements in ultrasound technologies.
