<p><p>The glaucoma laser therapies market encompasses various types of glaucoma treatments tailored to specific conditions. Chronic open-angle glaucoma, the most common form, is treated with selective laser trabeculoplasty to improve fluid drainage. Acute closed-angle glaucoma requires urgent laser iridotomy to relieve pressure. Secondary glaucoma treatments address underlying conditions causing increased eye pressure, while normal-tension glaucoma management focuses on methods to lower eye pressure despite normal readings. Each therapy targets unique mechanisms to mitigate vision loss and disease progression.</p></p>
