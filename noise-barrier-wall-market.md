<p><p>The noise barrier wall market is primarily divided into two types: ground-mounted and structure-mounted noise barriers. Ground-mounted noise barriers are installed on the ground, typically along highways or railway lines, effectively reducing noise pollution from vehicular traffic. In contrast, structure-mounted noise barriers are affixed to existing structures like buildings or bridges, targeting noise reduction in urban settings or dense residential areas. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing sound insulation and improving the quality of life in noisy environments.</p></p>
