<p><p>The General Surgery Tissue Adhesive market is primarily segmented into polyurethane-based and cyanoacrylate-based adhesives. Polyurethane-based adhesives offer flexibility and durability, making them suitable for a variety of tissue applications and often providing better adhesion in moist environments. In contrast, cyanoacrylate-based adhesives are favored for their rapid bonding capabilities and low toxicity, making them ideal for quick wound closure and minimal scarring. Each type serves specific surgical needs, ensuring effective tissue approximation and promoting healing.</p></p>
