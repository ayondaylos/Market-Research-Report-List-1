<p><ul><li>Mechanical Type</li><li>Fusion Splice Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Field Install Connector market encompasses two primary types: Mechanical Type and Fusion Splice Type. Mechanical Type connectors utilize physical alignment and crimping to join fibers, offering quick installation and minimal skill requirements. In contrast, Fusion Splice Type connectors apply heat to fuse fiber ends, ensuring low loss and high performance but requiring more specialized equipment and expertise. Both types cater to the rising demand for efficient, reliable connectivity in telecommunications, data centers, and various industrial applications.</p></p>
