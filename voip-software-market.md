<p><p>The VoIP software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based VoIP software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access services via the internet, offering scalability, easy updates, and lower maintenance costs. On-premises VoIP software, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company’s hardware, providing greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types cater to different business needs and infrastructure preferences.</p></p>
