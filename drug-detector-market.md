<p><p>The drug detector market includes various technologies for identifying illicit substances. X-ray drug detectors utilize high-energy radiation to visualize hidden drugs within luggage or cargo. MRI drug detectors employ magnetic resonance imaging to analyze the molecular structure of substances. Steam drug detectors utilize vapor analysis methods to identify trace amounts of drugs in various environments. Neutron drug detectors leverage neutron radiation to penetrate materials, revealing concealed drugs without damaging the samples. Together, these technologies enhance security and law enforcement efforts in combating drug trafficking.</p></p>
