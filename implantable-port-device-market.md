<p><p>The implantable port device market includes single lumen and dual lumen ports, each serving distinct medical needs. Single lumen implantable ports feature one channel for medication delivery or blood draws, ideal for patients requiring less frequent treatment. In contrast, dual lumen implantable ports have two separate channels, allowing simultaneous access for multiple therapies, reducing the need for repeated needle insertions. Both types enhance patient comfort and streamline care, catering to varying treatment regimens in oncology, intravenous therapy, and other medical specialties.</p></p>
