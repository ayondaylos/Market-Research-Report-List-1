<p><p>Air source heat pumps (ASHP) are systems that extract heat from the air to provide heating or cooling for residential and commercial buildings. The two primary market types are air-to-air and air-to-water. Air-to-air heat pumps transfer heat directly to the indoor air, making them ideal for space heating and cooling. In contrast, air-to-water heat pumps transfer heat to water, which can be used for underfloor heating or domestic hot water, catering to different heating needs and applications.</p></p>
