<p><p>The circular fire dampers market is categorized into manual and motorized fire dampers. Manual fire dampers are operated by hand and require physical intervention to close or open, providing a cost-effective solution for specific applications. In contrast, motorized fire dampers are automated devices equipped with electric or pneumatic actuators, allowing for remote operation and integration with fire alarm systems. Motorized dampers enhance safety by enabling faster response to fire conditions, making them increasingly popular in modern building designs.</p></p>
