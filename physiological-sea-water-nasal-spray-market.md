<p><p>The Physiological Sea Water Nasal Spray market is divided into two main types: isotonic and hypertonic. Isotonic physiological sea water nasal sprays maintain the same salt concentration as body fluids, helping to moisturize and clear nasal passages without causing irritation. In contrast, hypertonic physiological sea water nasal sprays have a higher salt concentration, effectively reducing nasal congestion and promoting mucus drainage, making them suitable for users dealing with sinusitis or allergies. Both types cater to different respiratory needs and preferences.</p></p>
