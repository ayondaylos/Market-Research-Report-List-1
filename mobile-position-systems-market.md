<p><ul><li>Indoor Type</li><li>Outdoor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile Positioning Systems (MPS) are categorized into two main types: indoor and outdoor. Outdoor MPS primarily rely on GPS technology, leveraging satellite signals to precisely determine locations in open spaces. This type is widely used in navigation and fleet management. In contrast, indoor MPS utilize technologies like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and RFID for accurate positioning within buildings where satellite signals are weak or unavailable. This type is essential for applications like indoor navigation, asset tracking, and location-based services in large venues.</p></p>
