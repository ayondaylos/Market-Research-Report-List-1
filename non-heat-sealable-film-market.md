<p><p>The Non Heat Sealable Film market is categorized into three types based on thickness: less than 12 microns, 12-30 microns, and more than 30 microns. Films less than 12 microns are ultra-thin, often used in applications requiring minimal weight and flexibility. Those between 12-30 microns offer a balance of durability and flexibility, making them suitable for packaging and protective applications. Films exceeding 30 microns are thicker and more robust, ideal for heavy-duty applications requiring enhanced mechanical strength and barrier properties.</p></p>
