<p><p>The Portable Numerical Control Cutting Machine market is primarily categorized into flame cutting and plasma cutting types. Flame cutting utilizes a high-temperature flame to melt and cut through materials, ideal for thicker metals. In contrast, plasma cutting employs a concentrated jet of hot plasma to sever metal with precision, suitable for various thicknesses and materials. Both methods are favored for their efficiency, accuracy, and versatility in metal fabrication industries, catering to diverse applications from construction to automotive manufacturing.</p></p>
