<p><ul><li>Shaft Screw Conveyor</li><li>Shaftless Screw Conveyor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screw conveyor equipment market features two main types: shaft screw conveyors and shaftless screw conveyors. Shaft screw conveyors utilize a helical screw blade around a central shaft to transport materials, making them suitable for a variety of bulk materials. In contrast, shaftless screw conveyors operate without a central shaft, allowing for the handling of sticky or challenging materials with reduced risk of clogging. Both types are widely used in industrial applications for efficient material handling and processing.</p></p>
