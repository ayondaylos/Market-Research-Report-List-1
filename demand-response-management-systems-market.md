<p><strong>The Demand Response Management Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Demand Response</li><li>Automated Demand Response</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Demand Response Management Systems (DRMS) encompass two primary market types: Conventional Demand Response and Automated Demand Response. Conventional Demand Response involves manual or pre-scheduled demand reductions, where consumers respond to utility requests for lower energy use during peak periods, often through incentives. In contrast, Automated Demand Response utilizes advanced technology to automatically adjust energy consumption based on real-time signals from the grid, enabling quicker and more efficient responses, enhancing reliability, and optimizing energy costs for both consumers and utilities.</p></p>
