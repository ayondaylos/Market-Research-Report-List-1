<p><p>Line voltage smart thermostats are designed to control heating systems powered directly by the electrical line voltage, typically 120V or 240V. Single pole wiring thermostats connect to one wire, allowing for basic on/off control, ideal for simple heating systems. In contrast, double pole wiring thermostats involve two wires, enabling more complex system control, including the ability to handle higher currents and enhance safety. Both types cater to different heating setups, providing efficiency and smart features for energy management.</p></p>
